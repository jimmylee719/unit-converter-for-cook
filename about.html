我們的目標 (Our Goal)

「餐飲單位換算小幫手」旨在提供一個快速、直觀且無干擾的單位換算工具，專為全球的餐飲專業人士與料理愛好者設計。我們相信，一個好的工具應該是簡單而強大的，讓您能專注於您的創作，而不是繁瑣的換算。

The "Culinary Unit Conversion Helper" aims to provide a fast, intuitive, and distraction-free unit conversion tool designed for culinary professionals and cooking enthusiasts worldwide. We believe a good tool should be simple yet powerful, allowing you to focus on your creations, not on tedious conversions.
