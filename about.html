關於我們 (About Us)

我們的使命 (Our Mission)

我們的使命是為全球的專業廚師、烘焙師與美食愛好者，提供一個最快速、直觀且準確的餐飲單位換算工具。我們發現在處理來自不同國家（尤其是亞洲與歐美）的食譜時，單位換算總是一個惱人且容易出錯的環節。因此，我們創造了這個小幫手，希望能為您的烹飪之旅掃除障礙。

Our mission is to provide the fastest, most intuitive, and accurate culinary unit conversion tool for professional chefs, bakers, and food enthusiasts worldwide. We found that unit conversion is often an annoying and error-prone step when dealing with recipes from different countries (especially between Asia, Europe, and America). Therefore, we created this helper to remove barriers on your culinary journey.
